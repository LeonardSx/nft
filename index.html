Frequently Asked Questions

BNB farming is a method of generating income through staking Binance Coin. Some projects allow users to deposit their BNB and earn rewards in the form of project tokens. To start farming BNB, you'll need a Metamask account and connect it to the farming platform you're using. Once connected, you can deposit your BNB and begin earning rewards based on the project's staking and reward structure.

Soldiers' boosters are tools that allow you to increase the speed of your farming process, which in turn can help you generate higher income. You can purchase them through our platform and connect them to your Metamask account to activate them automatically. The more powerful the booster, the faster you can generate income in the farming process.

To create a Metamask account, follow these steps:

1. Install the Metamask extension on your web browser.

2. Create a new Metamask account and save your recovery phrase in a safe place.

  • 3. Connect your Metamask account to our platform and start farming BNB.
